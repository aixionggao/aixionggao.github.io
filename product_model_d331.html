Product Description

The glue dispensing process is increasingly important in industrial production, with stricter demands for precision and efficiency. Traditional manual dispensing methods, reliant on human operation, can no longer meet industrial requirements and have been increasingly replaced by automated dispensing technologies.

The D331 Single-Station Silicone Dispensing Machine adopts a programmable teaching-style operation. It features high precision, flexibility, and adaptability, supporting various glue formulations to meet diverse needs while ensuring cleanliness and efficiency in production environments.
