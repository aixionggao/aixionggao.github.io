Product Description

The LT-D3030 Dual-Component Automatic Glue Dispensing Machine precisely handles A and B glues through independently controlled pressure pumps. Using MaiYu's proprietary glue dispensing system, it delivers glue from A and B inlets to the mixing head via individual pathways, ensuring a consistent and precise output.

The dynamic mixing process uses a multi-layered rotating blade structure inside a mixing tube, ensuring that A and B glue streams are continuously cut and uniformly mixed. The glue outlet is equipped with interchangeable needles of various diameters, offering versatility and guaranteeing precise dispensing quantities.

Key Features

  1. Equipped with a touchscreen Chinese interface for easy operation, capable of storing up to 100 dispensing settings.
  2. Adjustable glue dispensing speed, time, and pause duration ensures stable output without leakage.
  3. Supports point, line, curve, circle, and three-axis linkage for handling complex dispensing tasks.
  4. Reduces labor requirements, with one device replacing the workload of 5-6 operators.
  5. Proportional dispensing supports edge-to-edge dispensing, minimizing glue waste.
  6. Dual-tank design ensures A and B glues maintain stability over time, preventing material degradation.
  7. Independent control systems avoid external influences like air pressure fluctuations, ensuring consistent output quality.
  8. The mixing tank features liquid level sensors with automatic alarms for efficient material replenishment.
  9. Optional glue heating, vacuum de-airing, and defoaming modules support a wide range of dispensing processes.
